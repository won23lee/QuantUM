About

We welcome beginners and advanced learners alike. Expect intro talks by faculty, student-led discussions, career panels, lab tours, and movie nights. Our goal is to make quantum approachable, tangible, and fun.

Programs

Talks & Workshops

Intro-to-quantum sessions, guest lectures, and hands-on tutorials with open materials.

Community

Research & Study Nights

Peer-led problem solving, paper reading groups, and mentorship connections.

Careers

Industry & Grad Pathways

Panels with alumni and partners exploring quantum careers and graduate school.
